Hello! I am Maddie DeWitt.

I am a UX designer in Atlanta. I am passionate about working together to create a product users love and add to user’s delight.

My UX Skills

USER EMPATHY

Being a good listener is the first and most crucial step to advocate for the user and solve their problem. This can be done with an empathy map, journey map and user persona.

USER RESEARCH

My focus is to become an expert at determining who the user is. This comes from understanding their pain points, their needs and goals through surveys and interviews.

PROTOTYPING

After determining who the user is, I will sketch and design wireframes through Figma to build a prototype that is aiming to solve a user’s problem, which will be iterated on after usability testing.

Tools I use: Figma, Miro, Trello, Zoom, Slack, InVision, GitHub, paper/pencil sketching
