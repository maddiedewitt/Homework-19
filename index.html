Hello! I am Maddie DeWitt.

I am a UX designer in Atlanta and recent Georgia Tech graduate gaining a UX/UI Certificate. I am passionate about helping others. I highly value working together to create a product users love and add to user’s delight.

My UX Skills

USER EMPATHY

Being a good listener is the first and most crucial step to advocate for the user and solve their problem. This can be done with an empathy map, setting aside assumptions and focusing solely on the user to gain real insight.

USER RESEARCH

My focus is to become an expert at determining WHO the user is. This comes from understanding their pain points, their needs and goals. I first plan and then conduct surveys and interviews, using Zoom, collecting and analyzing this data through Miro to interpret a way to solve the user’s problem.

PROTOTYPING

After determining who the user is and gathering and analyzing data from interviews, I sketch and design wireframes through Figma to build a prototype that is aiming to solve a user’s problem. I can use this prototype to then complete usability testing and iterate.
